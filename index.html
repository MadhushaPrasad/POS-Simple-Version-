Discover Our Story

In the 1950s, all that stood in this iconic location was a car park with a small kiosk. Even then it was the best spot for uninterrupted panoramic views of Adelaide, with the address a tourist destination in itself. In april 1999, SkyHorizon as we know it today opened.
